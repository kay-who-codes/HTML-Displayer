Selected Superlatives

  • Which book has the most chapters? Psalms with 150 (and it’s not close; second place is Isaiah with merely 66)
  • Which book has the fewest chapters? Obadiah, Philemon, 2 John, 3 John, Jude tie with 1 each
  • Which book has the most verses? Psalms again with 2,461 (second place is Genesis with 1,533)
  • Which book has the fewest verses? 2 John with 13
  • Which book has the most words (English Standard Version)? Psalms again with 41,273 (but second place Isaiah is much closer this time with 40,544)
  • Which book has the fewest words (English Standard Version)? 2 John with 302 (narrowly beating 3 John with 303)

  1. I counted words as printed, so “22,000 cattle” counts as two words, not the three words in “twenty-two thousand cattle.” ↩
